Zilong Wang

I am a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at UC San Diego in 2025, advised by Professor Jingbo Shang. Before that, I received my B.S. from Peking University in 2019, where I worked with Professor Xiaojun Wan.

My research focuses on building effective and reliable LLM agents for code generation. If you'd like to discuss research—or just chat—feel free to reach out at zlwang.ucsd at gmail dot com .
