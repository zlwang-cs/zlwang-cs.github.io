Zilong Wang

I am a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind, where I work on agentic coding for Gemini, with a focus on cybersecurity and software engineering tasks. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at UC San Diego in 2025, advised by Professor Jingbo Shang. Before UCSD, I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Peking University in 2020, where I was fortunate to be advised by Professor Xiaojun Wan.

I am broadly interested in agentic RL, coding LLMs, and data synthesis for training capable language model agents. If you'd like to discuss research — or just chat — feel free to connect zlwang.ucsd [at] gmail [dot] com.
